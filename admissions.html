Seeking admission in one of the best schools in India? Here are the must-knows for a smooth admission process.

Please fill the online application form by clicking here. Parents can confirm admission to Podar International School either by visiting the School or Online.

a) Online

1) Complete the online application form.

2) Please ensure that the following documents are uploaded while filling the online application form.

a. Photocopy of the student’s birth certificate and Aadhaar Card (if applicable)
b. School leaving certificate (can be submitted later if not currently available)
c. UDISE Number (Unified District information System for Education) from current school
d. Photocopy of the previous and current class Report Card
e. Photocopy of Address proof and Parent's Pan Card
f. Passport size photograph of the student.

3) After submitting the online application form, if there are seats available in the standard that you have applied in, an email with a payment link for the first term fees will be sent to you. Parents are requested to make the necessary payment online.

b) Visiting the School

1) Complete the online application form.

2) Please submit the printed application form along with following documents at the School Office.

a. Photocopy of the student’s birth certificate and Aadhaar Card (if applicable).
b. School leaving certificate (can be submitted later if not currently available).
c. UDISE Number (Unified District information System for Education) from current school.
d. Photocopy of the previous and current class Report Card.
e. Photocopy of Address proof and Parent's Pan Card (original to be carried for verification).
f. 3 passport size photographs of the student.

3) Please submit a Cheque with the first term fees to the School Office. All payments at the School are to be made by Cheque. Please mention the name of your ward, and contact number on the rear side of the Cheque. Fees once paid will be strictly non-refundable.
