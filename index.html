ABOUT US

Educating 1,55,000 students through 124 schools across India, Podar Education Group has established itself as a leader in the Indian Education Sector. Established in 1927, Podar Education Group strives to create modern citizens who are ready to make global changes. The success of Podar is largely due to its trained, qualified and committed teaching team and the extensive use of technology in teaching.
